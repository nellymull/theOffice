Created by The Office's Mom, The Office is a deli restaurant and event saloon, located in the heart of Charlotte, NC. With the sole intention of bringing the deliciousness of mom's healthy food, the office has adapted to the pressure of the city life, and has developed a blend of quality, taste, and speed of service, while maintaining a cozy environment. Anyone is always welcomed at The Office Deli.

The Office Deli is also the perfect place for those professionals who are in a tight schedule, but wish to remain healthy, watch the figure, or simply have a break from the stress that the city life brings. If you are here for business or pleasure, The Office is a must visit place for lunch.
