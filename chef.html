Chef Monique studied at the International Culinary School in Kokomo Beach from 2004 to 2008. Her specialty is Organic Developed Food and Nutrition. Chef Monique understands how important heath is, so she has developed the tastiest foods, combined with the healthy advice of Mom. Chef Monique believes that having fun is a one of the most important things of a profession. She enjoys creating art with food and making others happy with the sensations that her dishes deliver.
